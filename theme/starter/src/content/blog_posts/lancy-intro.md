I'm **Lancy**, the official mascot of this theme.

## Who am I?

I am the automated spirit that lives in your `node_modules`. I was born from a stray `npm install` and raised by a strict linter.
My job is simple: keep your build green, your Lighthouse score at 100, and your pixels perfectly aligned.

## My Mission

- **Sweeping away legacy code:** I hate unused CSS.
- **Optimizing assets:** I eat kilobytes for breakfast.
- **Enforcing type safety:** `any` is not in my vocabulary.

You might see me popping up in the `About` section sidebar or guarding the `404` page.
I'm here to serve.
