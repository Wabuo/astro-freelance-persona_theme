Freelancing offers freedom, but it comes with responsibility.

## The Good

- Flexible schedule
- Choose your projects
- unlimited earning potential

## The Bad

- Inconsistent income
- No paid time off
- You are the boss, HR, and janitor

It's a wild ride, but I wouldn't trade it for anything.
