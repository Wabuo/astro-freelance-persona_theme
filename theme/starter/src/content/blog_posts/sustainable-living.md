## Why Permaculture?

Permaculture is more than just gardening; it's a design philosophy.

### Key Principles

1. **Observe and Interact**: By taking time to engage with nature we can design solutions that suit our particular situation.
2. **Catch and Store Energy**: By developing systems that collect resources when they are abundant, we can use them in times of need.
3. **Obtain a Yield**: Ensure that you are getting truly useful rewards as part of the work that you are doing.

## Getting Started

Start small. A herb spiral or a compost bin is a great first step.
